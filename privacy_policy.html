Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 21, 2025

1. Our Core Principle: Client-Side Processing

UtilityKit operates primarily on the principle of **client-side processing**. This means that for all core functionalities, such as image conversion, PDF manipulation, JSON formatting, and password generation, your files and data are processed **entirely within your web browser**. **Your files are never uploaded to our servers,** guaranteeing maximum privacy and security. We do not store, view, or transmit your processed data.

2. Information We Collect

Due to our client-side model, we collect very little direct personal information.

  • **Personal Data:** We do not require or collect any personally identifiable information (Name, Email, Address, etc.) to use our tools.
  • **Usage Data:** Like most websites, we may collect non-personal information such as your browser type, operating system, pages visited, and time spent on the site through third-party analytics (e.g., Google Analytics) to help us improve our service.

3. Third-Party Advertisers (Google AdSense)

UtilityKit uses Google AdSense to serve advertisements.

  • **Google as a Third-Party Vendor:** Google uses cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to this and other websites.
  • **The DoubleClick DART Cookie:** Google's use of the DART cookie enables it and its partners to serve ads to our users based on their visit to UtilityKit and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • **Opting Out:** Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google Ad and Content Network Privacy Policy at the following URL: http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html.
  • **Other Advertising Partners:** Our advertising partners may include other third-party vendors. We have no access to or control over these cookies that are used by third-party advertisers.

4. Links to Other Sites

Our service may contain links to other sites that are not operated by us. If you click on a third-party link, you will be directed to that third party's site. We strongly advise you to review the Privacy Policy of every site you visit.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@utilitykit.xyz.
